Privacy Policy for Sakeenah

Last updated: [INSERT DATE]

Sakeenah ("the app") is designed with your privacy as a first principle.

Data collection

Sakeenah does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

Specifically:

  • Text you type or speak stays on your device only. It is processed entirely within your browser/app to match a feeling theme, and is never sent to any server, database, or third party.
  • Voice input uses your device's built-in speech-to-text feature (provided by your browser or operating system). Audio is processed by your device/browser according to its own settings — Sakeenah itself never records, stores, or transmits audio.
  • No analytics, tracking, or advertising is used anywhere in this app.
  • No account, sign-up, or personal information is ever requested.
  • No internet connection is required to use the app after it is installed — all Qur'an and hadith content is bundled directly into the app itself.

Generated images

The "Generate image" feature creates an image entirely on your own device using your browser's built-in drawing tools. The image is saved directly to your device's downloads/photos. Sakeenah does not upload, store, or have access to any image you generate.

Third-party services

Sakeenah uses no third-party analytics, advertising, or data-processing services of any kind.

Children's privacy

Sakeenah does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children, because it does not collect information at all.
